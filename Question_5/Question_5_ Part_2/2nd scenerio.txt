Scenario: Enhanced Inspection and Documentation

Persona: Heena Sharma, Civil Engineer 

Goal: To check inspection documentation is right and can be easily managed 

Description: 

Heena sharma responsibility is to do routine inspection of bridges, roads, tunnels etc. She takes pictures, notes, check previous notes, sometimes different types 
of measurement also. She struggles always as she has stored here notes in different tools like laptop, mobile notes, sometimes she has to open her notebook. 
It is really time consuming to open different tools just to see a little bit information. Sometimes she read  wrong information which effects her perfomance also. 

User Story: 

Heena really want integrate her company audit software to note taking software (evernote) to store all her notes, photos etc. in one reliable platform so her 
performance will improve a lot.
